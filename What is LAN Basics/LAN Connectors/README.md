# LAN Connectors
# 📖 Overview

**LAN Connectors** are hardware components used to establish a physical connection between transmission media (network cables) and networking devices.

They play a critical role in ensuring reliable data transmission by securely connecting cables to devices such as computers, switches, routers, patch panels, and communication equipment.

Different transmission media require different connector types. Selecting the correct connector ensures compatibility, minimizes signal loss, and improves overall network reliability.

# 🔍 Why are LAN Connectors Important?

LAN Connectors are essential because they:

- Connect cables to networking devices
- Ensure stable and reliable communication
- Reduce signal loss
- Simplify cable installation and replacement
- Improve overall network performance

Without proper connectors, network devices cannot communicate effectively.

# 💡 Choosing the Right Connector

The appropriate connector depends on the transmission medium being used:

- **Coaxial Cable** → BNC Connector
- **Twisted Pair Cable** → RJ45 Connector
- **Telephone Cable** → RJ11 Connector
- **Fiber Optic Cable** → ST, SC, FC, or LC Connectors

Using the correct connector ensures optimal network performance and compatibility.
